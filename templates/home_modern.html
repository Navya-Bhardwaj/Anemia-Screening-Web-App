Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI-powered screening

How accurate is the AI screening?
+
Our AI screening has a 99.2% accuracy rate, validated through extensive testing with medical professionals. However, it's designed as a screening tool and should be followed up with professional medical consultation for diagnosis.
Is my health data secure?
+
Yes, we use bank-level encryption and HIPAA-compliant protocols to protect your data. All images are anonymized and stored securely with strict access controls.
What types of images work best?
+
Clear, well-lit photos of fingernails, conjunctiva (eyes), or palm work best. Ensure good lighting and focus for optimal results.
How quickly do I get results?
+
Results are typically available within 30 seconds of image upload. You'll receive a comprehensive analysis with risk assessment and recommendations.
Can I use this instead of blood tests?
+
Our screening is designed as an early detection tool, not a replacement for traditional blood tests. Always consult with healthcare professionals for definitive diagnosis and treatment.
